Hey there, I'm Henry J. E. Crookes.

A Front-End Web Developer by trade, U.K native and based in Berlin.

I enjoy obscure sci-fi & fantasy novels, tv shows about big robots, dogs wearing hats, and building kick-ass websites.

Thank you for checking out my site.

This is version 1.0 of my web-developer portfolio.
As such, I wanted to create a site that displayed all the essentials, but with only the basic tech required. This is only a static page, and throwing a bulky framework and extraneous libraries on top seemed excessive. Therefore, I wanted to showcase my solid grasp of the basic web-development skills, therefore this single-page site is built with pure HTML5, CSS3, Javascript and a touch of jQuery.

If you want to see my experience with newer, more impressive technologies such as Vue, React, Redux, Next.js, AWS etc. I believe my portfolio projects will speak for themselves.

Web Development is a challenge, and a creative outlet. I like to create web projects focussed on user interactivity, and unique graphics.

I love to explore creative fields such as music, animation, video and writing. I believe that designing for the web is the perfect focal point of all of my disciplines.

I have been coding for the Web since early 2019. In February 2020, I graduated from an intensive 3-month FullStack course at Spiced-Academy .

During the course I strengthened my fundamental web-design abilities and refined my skills in programming, team-collaboration and industry practices.

Since then I have gone on to work as a freelance developer on multiple exciting projects, all while picking up a host of new skills & experiences.

Since October 2020, I have been freelancing as the lead Front-End Developer for joinmorpheus, a small Berlin based startup, to help build their dream of being Germany's first truly narbon nuetral energy comparison site.

Working there has provided many challenges such as navigating a multi-lingual workspace & developing a flexibility for cross-discipline tasks.

joinmorpheus has given me a solid real-world experience of working in a development team environment and I'm excited for more.

Employing a diverse range of technologies, I create clean and responsive sites, and tools with dynamic effects.

I also work with VFX, using Adobe After Effects to create striking video with motion graphics. Finding new ways to use video for the web is one of my main interests.

Making a good site great is my end-goal.
